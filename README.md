ENABLE is our Second year research project exploring how university IT systems can work better for neurodivergent students. We're a team of five CS students at the University of Bradford, and we're spending this semester analyzing the current systems (like UoB Intranet and Canvas), talking to students, and figuring out what actually helps people with dyslexia, ADHD, autism, and other neurodivergent conditions. Instead of building something from scratch, we're researching what's missing, what works, and what the university could do differently to make everyone's life easier. This repo contains our literature review, gap analysis, meeting minutes with our supervisors (Prof. Neagu and Dr. Brown), and our final proposal. No code here - it's all research and documentation since that's the focus of our project.
